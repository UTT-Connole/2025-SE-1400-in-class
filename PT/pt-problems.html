Common Physical Therapy Complaints

Patient Concerns

  • Treatment not showing expected results
  • Exercises causing increased pain
  • Long wait times for appointments
  • Communication issues with therapist
  • Insurance coverage problems
  • Sessions feel rushed or impersonal

Treatment Issues

  • Lack of personalized treatment plans
  • Inadequate explanation of exercises
  • Equipment availability problems
  • Insufficient progress tracking
  • Limited one-on-one attention

How to Address Complaints

  • Communicate openly with your therapist
  • Request a re-evaluation of your treatment plan
  • Contact clinic management if needed
  • File formal complaints with licensing boards if necessary
  • Consider seeking a second opinion
