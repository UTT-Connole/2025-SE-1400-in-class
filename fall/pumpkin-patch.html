Your Local Fall Destination Since 1985

Welcome to Our Farm

Located just 15 minutes from downtown, Sunny Acres Pumpkin Patch offers the perfect fall experience for families. Our 50-acre farm features over 20 varieties of pumpkins, from tiny decorative gourds to giant jack-o'-lantern pumpkins weighing over 100 pounds!
