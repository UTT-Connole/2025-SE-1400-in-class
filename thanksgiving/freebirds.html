About the Movie

Free Birds is an animated comedy film about two turkeys who travel back in time to prevent turkey from becoming a Thanksgiving staple.

Plot

Reggie, a turkey pardoned by the President, teams up with Jake, a determined turkey, to use a time machine and journey to the first Thanksgiving. Their mission: change history and take turkey off the menu forever.

Cast

  • Owen Wilson as Reggie
  • Woody Harrelson as Jake
  • Amy Poehler as Jenny
  • George Takei as S.T.E.V.E.

Details

  • Release Date: November 1, 2013
  • Director: Jimmy Hayward
  • Genre: Animation, Adventure, Comedy
