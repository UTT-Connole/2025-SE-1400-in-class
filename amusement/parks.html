World's Best Amusement Parks

According to Ryan

  • Walt Disney World Resort

    Located in Orlando, Florida, Walt Disney World is the most visited vacation resort in the world, featuring four theme parks and two water parks.

  • Universal Studios Japan

    Situated in Osaka, Universal Studios Japan offers thrilling rides and attractions based on popular movies and franchises.

  • Europa-Park

    Located in Rust, Germany, Europa-Park is the largest theme park in Germany and is known for its European-themed areas and world-class roller coasters.

  • Tivoli Gardens

    Opened in 1843 in Copenhagen, Denmark, Tivoli Gardens is one of the world's oldest amusement parks, famous for its charm and classic rides.

  • Tokyo Disneyland

    Tokyo Disneyland, located in Urayasu, Japan, is renowned for its hospitality, unique attractions, and magical atmosphere.

  • Circus Circus

    It's super clean and family friendly

  • Disneyland Park

    Disneyland Park in Anaheim, California, is the original Disney theme park, opened in 1955 and beloved for its classic attractions.

  • Universal Studios Hollywood

    Located in Los Angeles, Universal Studios Hollywood combines a working movie studio with thrilling rides and shows.

  • Six Flags Magic Mountain

    Six Flags Magic Mountain in Valencia, California, is known for its record-breaking roller coasters and adrenaline-pumping rides.

  • PortAventura Park

    Located in Salou, Spain, PortAventura Park features themed areas and some of Europe's tallest and fastest roller coasters.

  • Everland

    Everland, in Yongin, South Korea, is the country's largest theme park, offering a mix of rides, a zoo, and seasonal festivals.

  • Alton Towers

    Alton Towers in Staffordshire, England, is famous for its innovative roller coasters and beautiful gardens.

  • Gardaland

    Gardaland, located near Lake Garda in Italy, is one of Europe's most popular theme parks, known for its family-friendly attractions.

  • Phantasialand

    Phantasialand in Brühl, Germany, offers immersive themed areas and world-class rides like Taron and Black Mamba.

  • Lotte World

    Lotte World in Seoul, South Korea, features the world’s largest indoor theme park and an outdoor amusement park.

  • Canada's Wonderland

    Located near Toronto, Canada's Wonderland is the largest amusement park in Canada, with over 200 attractions.

  • Ocean Park Hong Kong

    Ocean Park Hong Kong combines marine life exhibits, animal shows, and thrilling rides in a scenic setting.

  • Ferrari World Abu Dhabi

    Ferrari World on Yas Island, Abu Dhabi, is home to Formula Rossa, the world’s fastest roller coaster.

  • Busch Gardens Tampa Bay

    Busch Gardens in Tampa, Florida, is known for its African-themed rides, animal habitats, and thrilling coasters.

  • Efteling

    Efteling in Kaatsheuvel, Netherlands, is a fantasy-themed park inspired by fairy tales and folklore.

  • Blackpool Pleasure Beach

    Blackpool Pleasure Beach in England is a historic amusement park famous for its classic wooden coasters and seaside location.

  • Hong Kong Disneyland

    Hong Kong Disneyland blends Disney magic with local culture, offering unique attractions and entertainment.
